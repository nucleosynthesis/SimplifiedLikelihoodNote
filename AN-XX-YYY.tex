\abstract{In this note, a procedure for the construction of simplified likelihoods for 
the re-interpretation of public CMS searches for new physics is presented. The procedure relies on the 
use of a reduced set of information on the background models used in these searches 
which can readily be provided by the CMS collaboration. A toy example is used to demonstrate the 
procedure and its accuracy in reproducing full likelihood used by the CMS collaboration for setting 
limits in models for physics beyond the standard model.}
